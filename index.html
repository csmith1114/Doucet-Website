About Our Practice

Healthcare Rooted in Community

Founded in 2012 by Nicole Doucet, FNP, Doucet Family Healthcare began as a single clinic in Iota with a simple mission: bring quality, compassionate healthcare to rural Acadiana communities that need it most.

Today, we've grown to four Medicare-certified Rural Health Clinics across Southwest Louisiana — in Iota, Rayne, Jennings, and Pine Prairie — staffed by a team of dedicated nurse practitioners who treat patients like family. Alongside traditional primary care, we also offer functional and preventative medicine for patients seeking root-cause, whole-person wellness.

  • Four clinics across Acadiana
  • Medicare-certified Rural Health Clinics
  • Walk-ins welcome at all locations
  • Accepting new patients of all ages
