Services at Iota

🌱

Primary Care

Comprehensive family medicine for patients of all ages, from routine checkups to ongoing health management.

⚡

Walk-In Care

No appointment needed for urgent health concerns. Walk in during business hours for prompt evaluation and treatment.

👶

Pediatric Care

Well-child visits, developmental screenings, and childhood vaccinations in a family-friendly environment.

Ongoing care plans for diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and other chronic conditions.
